Experiment - 02 Magic Milk

Create colorful swirling patterns with milk, food coloring, and dish soap!

Material:

  • - Whole milk
  • - Food coloring
  • - Dish soap
  • - Cotton swab

Steps:

  1. Pour milk into a shallow dish.
  2. Add a few drops of different food coloring.
  3. Dip a cotton swab into dish soap, then touch it to the center of the milk.
  4. Watch the colors swirl and dance!
