Experiment - 04 Rainbow in a Jar

Create a colorful rainbow using liquids of different densities.

Material:

  • - A clear jar or glass
  • - Honey
  • - Dish soap
  • - Water
  • - Vegetable oil
  • - Rubbing alcohol
  • - Food coloring

Steps:

  1. Add a layer of honey at the bottom of the jar.
  2. Slowly add each liquid in order, coloring the water and rubbing alcohol with food coloring.
  3. Pour each liquid gently to keep the layers separate.
  4. Observe the beautiful rainbow of liquids!
