Experiment - 03 DIY Lava Lamp

Make a simple lava lamp with oil, water, and effervescent tablets!

Material:

  • - Clear bottle or jar
  • - Water
  • - Vegetable oil
  • - Food coloring
  • - Alka-Seltzer or fizzy tablets

Steps:

  1. Fill the bottle halfway with water.
  2. Add a few drops of food coloring.
  3. Top it up with vegetable oil.
  4. Drop an effervescent tablet into the bottle.
  5. Watch the bubbles create a lava lamp effect!
