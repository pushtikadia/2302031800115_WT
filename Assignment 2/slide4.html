Experiment - 01 Baking Soda Volcano Eruption

Watch a chemical reaction unfold with this classic volcano experiment!

Material:

  • - Baking soda
  • - Vinegar
  • - A plastic bottle
  • - Food coloring (optional)
  • - Tray to catch the mess

Steps:

  1. Place the bottle in the tray.
  2. Fill the bottle halfway with baking soda.
  3. Add a few drops of food coloring.
  4. Pour vinegar into the bottle and watch it erupt!
